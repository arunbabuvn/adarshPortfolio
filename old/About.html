Hello! I'm Adarsh, a UX designer from a small village in Kerala, India. I'm currently studying Computer Science and Engineering at Government Engineering College Thrissur.


I started off with frontend web development, and then switched to UI design. I've found that I really enjoy designing user centric interfaces rather than coding.


Now I'm working as a user interface designer intern at GTech Mulearn. where we're trying to decentralize, democratize, demonetize, and digitize learning to equip passionate with future skills.


I'm involved in UX design at the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) Government Engineering College Chapter.


Additionally, I co-founded a GitHub community called Schematic Bytes. It's a group of software developers and UX designers working together to solve real-world problems and making the world a better place.
